Dany Boon

Birth date: 26.06.1966
Birth place: Armentieres, France

Dani Boon is a French comedian, director, and film and theater actor.
At first, Dani Boone made a living by voicing cartoons and performing pantomimes on the streets. In 1992, he made a breakthrough in his career as a comedian: French TV host Patrick Sebastian invited Danny to his program. Since then, Boone has been gaining popularity through his comedy miniatures and his own humorous concerts.

Boon is very fond of his small homeland, namely, the Nord-Pas-de-Calais region. In 2003, he even held one of his concerts, speaking a local dialect called Picard. In addition to using the dialect of the locals, Boone managed to sell 600,000 copies of DVDs of his concert (with subtitles) across the country.
